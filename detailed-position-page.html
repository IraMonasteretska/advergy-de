IT System Engineer

For our well-known customer, who is active in the fiber and paper industry, we are currently looking for an IT system administrator for a permanent position in the Aalen area.

The global company has been manufacturing sustainable products for over 100 years. With more than 3,000 employees at over 50 locations worldwide, it is financially stable and has high growth dynamics. Here you take on responsibility and can take your own initiatives without being slowed down by a lot of bureaucracy.

You will be part of an international team with 33 employees and have the opportunity to work in a future-oriented industry. We are looking for a committed person to fill our team.

Aufgaben

  • You configure and further develop the endpoint management systems.
  • You are responsible for software distribution and patch management
  • You look after and manage the IT systems
  • Carry out installation and configuration work
  • 1st & 2nd level support

Profil

  • Work experience in the IT sector
  • Competence in dealing with Microsoft products – Windows, Office, Outlook and SharePoint

Gehaltsrange

Approximate salary 4000-6000$*
*Dieser Wert ist lediglich ein Richtwert und variiert je nach Erfahrung sowie Qualifikationen.
